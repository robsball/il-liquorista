What has been said regarding paper filtration with glass funnels applies to small-scale operations. When, instead, larger quantities of liquor are to be processed, large cone-shaped filters made of tinned copper sheet are used, fitted with taps at the apex and fed from a reservoir likewise equipped with regulating taps.
