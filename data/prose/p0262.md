## Artificial Fruit Ethers and Essences

Under the name of ether or essence of this or that fruit, one finds in commerce mixtures of artificial chemical products (ethers or salts of organic acids) in alcoholic solution, whose odor closely resembles that of genuine fruits. Such artificial essences are now widely used, especially in confectionery and liqueurs.

Genuine fruit essences are very rarely found in commerce, as in general it is preferred to use the fruits directly.

Germany is one of the principal centers for the manufacture of artificial fruit essences, and such products should logically have greater sales in the northern countries than with us, where fruits find all the best conditions for production—except for human care!
