# Foreign Weights and Measures (continued): Sweden, Switzerland, Turkey, and Others

### Sweden

Decimal metric system; the following are still in use:

**Weights**

| Unit | Equivalent |
|---|---|
| Centner = 100 Pund | = 42.5075 kg |
| Pund = 100 Ort | = 0.425 kg |

### Switzerland

Decimal metric system; the following are still in use:

**Weights**

| Unit | Equivalent |
|---|---|
| Centner = 100 Pfund | = 50 kg |
| Pfund = 16 Unzen = 32 Loth | = 500 g |

**Capacity measures**

| Unit | Equivalent |
|---|---|
| Saum = 4 Eimer = 100 Maas | = 15 liters |
| Maas (Pot) = 2 Schoppe | = 1.500 liters |
| Schoppe | = 0.750 liters |

### Turkey

Decimal metric system; the unit names differ:

| Unit | Equivalent |
|---|---|
| Kilo à Cheky *(OCR uncertain)* | = 100 liters |
| Eulscée = 10 Zarf *(OCR uncertain)* | = 1 liter |
| Cantar à Cheky *(OCR uncertain)* | = 100 kg |
| Vecchia Oca *(OCR uncertain)* | = 1 kg |

Old measures still in use:

**Weights**

| Unit | Equivalent |
|---|---|
| Carico = 100 Oca | = 128.103 kg |
| Cantar = 44 Oca = 100 Rotoli | = 56.365 kg |
| Oca | = 1.281 kg |
| Rotolo | = 0.5636 kg |

### Other Foreign Measures of Frequent Use

Worth noting also: the Medana = 8 Cuba = 8.1272 liters, used in Abyssinia; the Cantar = 36 Oca = 100 Rottel = 44.473 kg (Rottel = 144 Dirhem = 444.7 g), used in Egypt; the Saa = 4 Mud = 55.501 liters, used in Morocco; and the Bina = 4 Meltar = 39.380 liters, used in Tunisia, etc.
