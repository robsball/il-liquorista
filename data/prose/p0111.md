# Classification of Vegetable Substances by Flavor and Aroma

...purely aromatic is the taste produced by an essential oil, and not simultaneously by a bitter substance.

We will now give a classification — or, to put it better, a grouping — of the various plant substances according to the taste and aroma they present. The indications in this list will serve as a sufficient guide for the Liquorista to establish in advance the approximate or prevailing flavor of the product he intends to obtain.

To avoid complicating this list, we will not subdivide it into separate categories of plants, roots, flowers, etc. taken individually, but will simply list them together within each group; this enumeration spares us further explanation:

**Very bitter taste.** — Wormwood, White Agaric, Quassia (wood), Aloe.

**Intense bitter taste.** — Blessed thistle, Water trefoil (bogbean), Cinchona bark, Colombo root, Gentian root, Centaury, Masterwort (Imperatoria).

**Less intense but aromatic bitter taste.** — Angostura (bark), bitter orange peel, Iva (ground-pine), hop flowers, Bergamot, Rhubarb root, Scabious, Ginger.

**Intense aroma, without specified bitterness.** — [root of "Untila Camp.", uncertain reading], Angelica (seeds), Valerian (root), Costmary (Balsamita), Benzoin, Serpentary (root), Sweet flag / Calamus root, Citron and Lemon peel, Roman chamomile, Coriander, Cumin, Caraway, Oregano, Curly mint, Bay (leaves), Marjoram, Sage, Orange blossom, Peppermint, Rosemary (leaves), Sassafras (wood), Thyme, Juniper (berries), Zedoary (root).

**Sweetish aroma, without specified bitterness.** — Musk-mallow seed (Ambretta), Arnica (flowers), Cacao, Common chamomile, Lemon balm (Melissa), Elderflower, Hyssop, Lavender (flowers), Sage, Tormentil (root), Violets, Orris (root), St. John's Wort.

**Intense aroma, without bitterness.** — Coca (leaves), Lovage (wild celery), Guaiacum, Kola (nuts), Licorice, Mastic (resin), Bittersweet nightshade (Dulcamara), Nutmeg, Mace, Tea.

**Pungent / burning taste.** — Pyrethrum (root), Galangal (root), Cardamom (Grains of Paradise), Arum (root), Cinnamon, Cloves.

**Vanilla or coumarin aroma.** — Melilot, Woodruff, Tonka beans, Vanilla.
