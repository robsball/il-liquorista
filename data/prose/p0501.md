# General Notes on Liqueur Composition and Extraction Methods

## General Notes on Liqueur Composition and Extraction Methods

Note that in addition to the special recipes we shall present, liqueurs made in general by extraction prepared through maceration or digestion (see Special Chapter) employ 1 kg to obtain 40–50 liters of liqueur, adding it to that quantity of Excipient or Simple Liqueur: conversely, for blends of essences that have not been previously diluted in alcohol (pp. 239–251), one-tenth is employed, that is, 100 grams per 40–50 liters.

The following blends yield 12 liters of liqueur when added to 12 liters of simple liqueur, or, to express it better, to the quantity of simple liqueur necessary to complete 12 liters of product.

In preparing the mixture, it will be appropriate to dilute the blend of essential oils or essences with alcohol, deducting its quantity from that indicated for the composition of the simple liqueur (see p. 106).

**N.B.** — It is understood therefore that in all the following recipes we have omitted the indication of the quantity of simple liqueur, which is to be considered for all practically the same, and namely such as to complete 12 liters of product.

### Acqua degli Amici (Friends' Water)

| Ingredient | Amount | Unit |
|---|---|---|
| Cognac extract | 5 | g |
| Essential oil of bergamot | 2 | g |
| Essential oil of lemon | 4 | g |
| Iris tincture | 10 | g |
| Vanilla tincture | 10 | g |

**Brown.**

### Acqua di Ardelle (Ardelle Water)

| Ingredient | Amount | Unit |
|---|---|---|
| Essential oil of lemon | 2 | g |
| Essential oil of cloves | 1 | g |
| Essential oil of nutmeg | 1 | g |
| Iris tincture | 50 | g |

**Violet.**

### Acqua d'Argento (Silver Water)

| Ingredient | Amount | Unit |
|---|---|---|
| Essential oil of bitter almond | 8 | g |
| Essential oil of lemon | 0.5 | g |
| Essential oil of nutmeg | 0.5 | g |
| Silver leaf, beaten fine | q.b. | — |

Add a few small pieces of beaten silver leaf, well minced.
