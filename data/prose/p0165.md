After what has already been said regarding the previously described stills, we do not believe further explanation of this one is necessary; suffice it to note that the cucurbit (retort) containing the material to be distilled is immersed in the boiler, which contains water or another liquid making up the water bath.

Water-bath distillation does not require as much care as distillation over an open flame; however, one must take the precaution of frequently cooling the condenser and of not distilling the liquid further than is appropriate.
