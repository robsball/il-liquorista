# Preparation of Colors (Pastes and Solutions)

## Storage and Handling

Paste colors must be protected from moisture; otherwise they become mucilaginous. Dry colors should be stored in a dry place.

During preparation, all colors must be stirred only with wooden, horn, or ivory agitators.

## Yellow (Giallo)

1. Digest 100 grams of saffron in 1 liter of alcohol; after 48 hours filter and treat the residue again with ½ liter of alcohol. Combine the liquids, filter the tincture, and mix with simple syrup.

2. Digest 340 grams of safflower for several days in 1½ liters of water; filter the liquid through flannel and mix with 250 grams of sugar syrup and 250 grams of gum solution.

3. Make a decoction of 1 kg of turmeric with 24 liters of water; strain and concentrate the liquid to 8 liters, to which add 410 grams of powdered crystallized alum, 2 kg of sugar syrup, and 750 grams of gum solution.
