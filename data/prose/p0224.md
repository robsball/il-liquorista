**Method:** Macerate the ground botanicals for 48 hours in the alcohol, then distill 20 liters. Add 10 liters of water to the distillate and rectify. These alcoholates are used to prepare the respective liqueur by mixing with other ingredients and sweetening as indicated in Part III (Liqueur Manufacture).

## Bitter Orange (Curaçao)

900 grams of dried bitter orange peel are immersed in sufficient water and freed of the white pith. Mix 1.250 kg of bitter orange peel (curaçao), called Garton, and digest in the still with 20 liters of alcohol. Distill and rectify with 5 liters of water to obtain finally 17 liters of product.
