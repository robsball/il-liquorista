## Extracts by Maceration or Direct Digestion of Plant Materials

In the following recipes, either the maceration method or the digestion method may be followed.

The denominations always refer to the determined property or various characteristics of the liqueur composed by means of the indicated extract.

The quantities of the various substances, unless otherwise stated, are expressed in grams.
